### Punishment for Murder
According to the **Indian Penal Code, Section 302**, any individual found guilty of committing murder is subject to the following penalties:
*   **Imprisonment:** The offender may be sentenced to death or imprisonment for life.
*   **Fine:** In addition to the term of imprisonment, the offender is also liable to pay a fine.

*   **Imprisonment:** The offender may be punished with imprisonment for life or with imprisonment of either description for a term that may extend up to ten years.
*   **Fine:** The offender is also liable to pay a fine.

In summary, while murder carries a potential sentence of death or life imprisonment, culpable homicide not amounting to murder carries a maximum imprisonment term of ten years or life imprisonment, depending on the circumstances of the case. Both offences mandate the imposition of a fine.
